units-
1) Pixels - fixed(px)
2) Percentage - dependent on parent(%)
3) Viewport width - dependent on device screen(vw)
4) Viewport height - dependent on device screen(vh)
5) Viewport maximum - dependent on maximum of viewport's height or width(vmax)
6) Viewport minimum - dependent on minimum of viewport's height or width(vmin)
7) em - dependent on parent size, child vary accordingly can be controlled by parent
8) rem - dependent on root element like <html>, child varies accordingly[1 rem=16px] can be controlled by root
